What's included in the price?

The price includes filing a single-member LLC and S Corp tax election, getting your EIN number, monthly bookkeeping services and financial reporting. You also get your very own accounting and tax advisor, who will file your business taxes and give you year-round tax support. All Spider plans include monthly bookkeeping and payroll.

What's not included in the price?

The price doesn't include third-party fees imposed by your city, state, or the federal government, as well as business license filing fees and other city-specific charges. Individual tax return preparation can be purchased for an additional one-time fee, and will be prepared by our integrated partner, TaxFyle.

Does Spider have a setup fee?

Yes, there is a one-time Onboarding fee of $199. This fee goes toward ensuring that your business is set up correctly, registered with the appropriate agencies, and in compliance with payroll requirements. This fee also covers catching up and cleaning up your year-to-date bookkeeping, regardless of the number of transactions.

What's Spider?

Spider is the first back-office system designed for self-employed business owners that lets them focus on their passion, not their paperwork.

What services does Spider offer?

Your Spider membership includes entity formation (if applicable), S Corp tax election, monthly bookkeeping and financial statements, payroll support and tax filing, as well as federal and state business tax returns and quarterly tax support. You can also purchase individual tax return preparation from our integrated partner, TaxFyle, for an additional, one-time fee.

How does Spider help with tax savings?

Your team of advisors is here to help you get the most out of your S Corp. We ensure that your bookkeeping is up to date and that your personalized tax advice is based on real, accurate numbers.

Even though we'd love to help everyone, our goal is to assist those who'd benefit the most from our services. Full-time solopreneurs, like marketing consultants, coaches, designers, software developers, and creatives who make over $60,000/year, find the most value in Spider.
